Bulalo

Bulalo

Dinner option

1 kg beef shank with bone marrow
1 corn, cut into pieces
1 small cabbage, quartered
1 onion, sliced
2 liters water
Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Boil the Beef: In a large pot, boil beef shank in water. Skim off any impurities.
  2. Add the Corn and Onion: Once the beef is tender, add corn and onion. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Add the Cabbage: Add cabbage and season with salt and pepper. Simmer for another 10 minutes.
  4. Serve: Serve hot with rice and a dipping sauce of fish sauce and calamansi.
