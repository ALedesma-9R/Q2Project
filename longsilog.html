Longsilog

Longsilog

Breakfast option

6 pieces Vigan or Lucban longganisa
4 cups cooked rice
4 cloves garlic, minced
4 eggs
Salt and pepper to taste
Cooking oil
  1. Cook the Longganisa: In a pan, add a small amount of water and the longganisa. Simmer until the water evaporates, then add a little oil to fry the longganisa until golden brown.
  2. Prepare the Sinangag (Garlic Fried Rice): In the same pan, add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant. Add the cooked rice, season with salt, and stir-fry for a few minutes.
  3. Fry the Eggs: In a separate pan, fry the eggs to your preferred doneness.
  4. Serve: Plate the longganisa, sinangag, and egg together for a delicious Luzon breakfast.
