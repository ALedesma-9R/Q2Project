Pastil

Pastil

Dessert option

2 cups glutinous rice
1 cup coconut jam or kalamay
Banana leaves
  1. Cook the Rice: Cook the glutinous rice.
  2. Wrap in Banana Leaves: Place a portion of rice on banana leaves and add coconut jam.
  3. Serve: Roll the banana leaf to wrap the rice. Serve warm.
