Bibingka

Bibingka

Dessert option

1 cup rice flour
1/2 cup coconut milk
1/4 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 salted egg, sliced
Banana leaves
Grated coconut for topping
  1. Prepare the Batter: Mix rice flour, coconut milk, sugar, and eggs in a bowl until smooth.
  2. Line the Molds: Place banana leaves in a baking pan or mold.
  3. Pour the Batter: Pour the batter into the molds and top with sliced salted egg.
  4. Bake: Bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for 20-25 minutes.
  5. Serve: Top with grated coconut before serving.
