Adobo

Adobo

Classic Filipino dish

2 lbs chicken, cut into pieces
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup vinegar
1 onion, chopped
1 head garlic, minced
2 bay leaves
1 tsp peppercorns
1 tbsp sugar
1/4 cup water
  1. Marinate the chicken in soy sauce, vinegar, onion, garlic, and bay leaves for at least an hour.
  2. In a pot, sauté the marinated chicken with its marinade until fragrant.
  3. Add water, sugar, and peppercorns, then simmer until the chicken is tender and the sauce has thickened.
  4. Serve hot with rice!
