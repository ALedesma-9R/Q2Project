Sinigang na Baboy

Sinigang na Baboy

Savory and tangy pork soup

2 lbs pork belly, cut into chunks
1 onion, quartered
2 tomatoes, quartered
1 eggplant, sliced
2 long green beans, cut into pieces
1 radish, sliced
1-2 cups kangkong (water spinach)
2 tbsp fish sauce
1-2 tbsp tamarind paste or 3-4 fresh tamarind pods
Salt to taste
Water
  1. Boil pork belly with onion and tomatoes in water for about 45 minutes, or until tender.
  2. Add the tamarind paste or fresh tamarind pods and continue boiling until the sourness is strong.
  3. Put in the eggplant, green beans, and radish. Let them cook until tender.
  4. Season with fish sauce and salt. Add the kangkong last and cook for another 2 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with rice!
